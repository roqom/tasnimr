If you feel overwhelmed on starting a healthy lifestyle, the best activity to begin with is walking. Don't think about jogging or running or brisk walking. Just walk normally. But walk for 20 minutes.

Why 20 minutes? Because study has shown that 20 minutes is enough time to get your heart rate up and you can reap significant benefits.
