Something seems simple yet it's complex. Like brewing a cup of coffee. It may look like it's about pouring hot water on the grinded beans, wait for the water to filter into a cup and done. 

Yet for  coffee experts, there are many subtleties and technicalities they know and mastered in order to brew a good cup of coffee.

There are subtleties not just in brewing coffee, but many things in life. And when we understand this subtleties and master it, a simple activity becomes beautiful.
