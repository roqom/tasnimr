Something may seem simple, yet it is complex.

Like brewing a cup of coffee. It may look like it’s just about pouring hot water over ground beans, waiting for the water to filter into a cup, and that’s it.

Yet for coffee experts, there are many subtleties and technicalities they understand and have mastered in order to brew a good cup of coffee.

There are subtleties not just in brewing coffee, but in many things in life. And when we understand these subtleties and master them, a simple activity becomes something beautiful and profound.
